# DfT Journey time statistics: data tables (JTS)

Data on journey times by LSOA and local authority.

## Journey times to key services by lower super output area

Health Services:
- JTS0505: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for GPs by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England
- JTS0506: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for Hospitals by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England
- JTS0509: Travel time, destination and origin indicators to Pharmacies by cycle and car, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England

Also for classifying rural vs urban area:
- JTS0501: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for Employment centres by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England
- JTS0502: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for Primary schools by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England
- JTS0503: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for Secondary schools by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England 
- JTS0504: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for Further education by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England
- JTS0508: Travel time, destination and origin indicators for town centres by mode of travel, Lower Super Output Area (LSOA), England
- JTS0930: Average travel time to city centres: England
